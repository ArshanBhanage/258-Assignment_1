# Flashcard Forge – 1-Minute Demo Script

## Opening (0:00–0:10)
> "Hi, this is Flashcard Forge — an AI-powered flashcard app built with Next.js, Firebase, and Google Gemini. Let me show you how it works."

**Action:** Show the landing page at `https://assignment-1b-ebcc3.web.app`

---

## Sign Up & Login (0:10–0:20)
> "First, I'll create an account. Just an email and password — Firebase Auth handles everything securely."

**Action:** Click **Get Started Free**, fill in email/password, submit. Dashboard loads showing an empty state.

---

## Create a Deck (0:20–0:35)
> "Now I'll create a flashcard deck. I'll type in a topic — say, 'React Hooks' — set the difficulty to Intermediate, choose 10 cards, and hit Generate. Gemini AI creates the entire deck in seconds."

**Action:** Click **Create New Deck**, enter topic "React Hooks", select Intermediate, 10 cards, click **Generate Deck**. Wait for cards to appear.

---

## View Deck & Cards (0:35–0:42)
> "Here's the generated deck with 10 Q&A cards. Each card has a question, answer, and a hint."

**Action:** Scroll through the card list briefly.

---

## Study Mode (0:42–0:55)
> "Let's study. Click a card to flip it, then mark whether I knew the answer. A progress bar tracks my session score in real time."

**Action:** Click **Study**, flip 2–3 cards, mark some correct and some incorrect, show the progress bar updating.

---

## Wrap Up (0:55–1:00)
> "That's Flashcard Forge — AI-generated decks, real-time mastery tracking, all deployed on Firebase. Thanks for watching!"

**Action:** Return to dashboard showing the deck with its mastery percentage.
