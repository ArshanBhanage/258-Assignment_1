# Receipt2Split - Demo Walkthrough Script

**Video Length**: ~1-2 minutes

## sc 1: Intro (Camera on Host or App Home Screen)
- "Hi, I'm [Name]. This is Receipt2Split, a Flutter app powered by Google Gemini that makes splitting bills painless."
- Show: Empty Home Screen.

## sc 2: Scanning a Receipt
- "Let's say we just had lunch. I'll tap 'New Split'."
- Action: Tap **New Split**.
- "I can snap a photo, but for this demo, I'll use the builtin Demo Receipt."
- Action: Tap **Try with Demo Receipt**.
- "Gemini is now analyzing the image to extract the merchant, items, and prices..."
- Show: Loading spinner.

## sc 3: Review & Editing
- Action: App transitions to **Review Screen**.
- "And there it is! 'The Smoky Grill'. All items extracted correctly: Burger, Fries, Coke."
- "The math looks right, so I'll continue."
- Action: Tap **Looks Good**.

## sc 4: Adding Friends
- Action: On **Participants Screen**.
- "I had lunch with Alice and Bob."
- Action: Type "Alice", tap Add. Type "Bob", tap Add.
- "Now let's see who ordered what."
- Action: Tap **Next**.

## sc 5: Assigning Items
- Action: On **Assign Screen**.
- "I (Me) had the Burger. Alice had the Fries. Bob and I shared the Coke."
- Action: Tap items to assign them to people.
- "Notice how easy it is to split shared items."
- Action: Tap **Calculate Split**.

## sc 6: Summary & Save
- Action: On **Summary Screen**.
- "Here's the breakdown. The app automatically split the tax and tip proportionally based on what everyone ordered."
- "Alice owes \$X, Bob owes \$Y."
- Action: Tap **Save & Finish**.
- Show: Home screen with the new entry in history.

## sc 7: Outro
- "That's Receipt2Split. Built with Flutter and Gemini. Thanks for watching!"
